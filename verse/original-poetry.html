Divided Lines

Written December 2024 - Inspired by contemporary political discourse

We stand on opposite shores
of a river that once was narrow,
now widened by the erosion
of patience and understanding.

Your truth and mine—
they used to overlap,
like colors bleeding into one another,
creating something new.

Now we shout across the water,
our words lost in the current,
each convinced the other
has forgotten how to listen.

But what if we both waded in?
Met in the middle, knee-deep
in the uncomfortable truth
that neither shore holds all the answers?
